| 14                 | iOS 7, iOS 8               | arm, arm64             | Go 1.5        |          |

| 12                 | OS X Mountain Lion 10.8    | amd64, 386             | Go 1 _*_      |          |

| 10                 | Mac OS X Snow Leopard 10.6 | amd64, 386             | Go 1          | Go 1.4.3 |
